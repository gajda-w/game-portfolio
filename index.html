About website

The portfolio I created is not just a showcase of my approach to web design. It's also an interactive game inspired by old RPG games, where the user can explore the map and get to know my projects, skills, and work style. I wanted my portfolio to be unique and creative, so I used different solutions than those found in most portfolios. I used technologies such as Typescript, HTML, CSS, and ThreeJS, but to achieve an even better effect, I used artificial intelligence to generate character graphics, sounds, and avatars. This made my website more unique and attractive. If you're interested in my approach to web design and would like to learn more details, I'd be happy to talk to you about it.
